# Project Statement: Single-File BMI Tracker and History Logger

## Problem Statement

The issue this project address is the inconvenience associated with personal health tracking.

# Whats the problem?

Its hard to keep the track of health info of a person. people usually use online bmi calculator once and forgot the record of data measured. You cant really see how easily your health is changing over the course of time.
What we need is a basic tool you can use from computer to figure out your BMI and save all your past records in one spot so you can easily look back and see and plan the future fix the issue of your health whenever you want.

# What this project does ?
 This project is just one file that does everything a person expect from BMI calculator. 
It is meant for one/multiple person and is made of only one python script . 

## Here is wqhat it will do:

* Figure out your BMI using the standard formula.
* Sort your BMI into records and keep them recorded
* Save your BMI measurements info in a text file on the computer


## Here is what it will not do
This project wont connect to internet , ask you to log in , show fancy charts .

## who is this for? 

This tool is for 
* Students and new coders who want to practice using python working wityh files and handling mistakes.
* People who want simple , private way to track their process without any fear of getting the info/data public or sharing their info with suspicious apps 
* people who need to figure out and records BMI for small people or group using simple commands


# main things it can do 

this thing does 3 jobs 

1. Calculate BMI
	you type you name, weight(KG) , height(cm)
2. Save data 
	
    *   It takes your name, BMI, category, and the time.
    *   It puts all that info into a line of text.
    *   It adds that line to a file called bmi\_history.txt and tells you it saved it.
3.  Show History

    *   You pick option 2 from the menu.
    *   It reads every line in the bmi\_history.txt file.

    *   It shows you all your past BMI records on the screen.
